About Us
At M. Berin Dental Clinic, located in the heart of Las Pi√±as City, we are dedicated to delivering top-notch dental services with a focus on affordability and accessibility. Our clinic is conveniently situated, making it easy for you to receive quality dental care without extensive travel.

We offer a wide range of dental services, including consultation, oral prophylaxis (cleaning), tooth filling, tooth extraction, wisdom tooth removal, mouthguards, root canal treatment, complete and partial dentures, retainers, braces, tooth whitening, dental sealants, and periapical radiographs.

Our friendly and accommodating team strives to create a positive and welcoming environment for both our clients and employees. We are committed to using only the finest materials and advanced techniques, ensuring that your dental health is in expert hands.
